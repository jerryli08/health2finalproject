Don't Ball Up Your Stress

How procrastination and social media are destroying our mental health, and what to do about it

Section 1: Academic Stress Is at a Breaking Point

For teenagers in high school, academic stress has reached historically high levels.

Procrastination is when someone voluntarily delays a task even though they know that delaying it will make the situation worse.

Many students procrastinate to the point it affects their grades and wellbeing.

What Stress Does to Your Brain

When you're stressed, your brain floods your body with cortisol.

Section 2: Your Phone Is Designed to Steal Your Focus

Social media is all engineered specifically to grab your attention and keep you unfocused.

How Dopamine Can Trap You

Every single notification, new post, or like you see triggers dopamine, a chemical neurotransmitter in your brain.

Anxiety and Self-Comparison

Comparing your life to others online increases anxiety and lowers self-esteem.
